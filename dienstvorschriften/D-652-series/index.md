# D 652 Series — Master Index (Dienstvorschriften)

**Panzerkampfwagen III · Panzerbefehlswagen · StuG / StuH (Gepanzerte Selbstfahrlafette f. Sturmgeschütz 7,5 cm Kanone)**

The **D 652** series is a single shared numbering scheme, because the StuG was built on the Panzer III chassis. The assault-gun manuals therefore have no series of their own: they are interleaved among the Panzer III manuals by subsystem (chassis, engine, running gear, armament, loading plan, maintenance). The weapon designation evolves within the series itself: from *7,5 cm Kanone* (Ausf. A–E) to *7,5 cm Sturmgeschütz 40* (from Ausf. F onward).

Primary source: Museum Alkmaar '40-'45 catalogue; cross-checked against the official superborg index and the Bushmakow restoration archive. The `+` suffix marks a classified (*geheim*) version. **NfD** = *Nur für den Dienstgebrauch* (restricted / for official use only). Each entry lists the original German title followed by the English translation.

## Project notes

- **No closed total count exists.** The D 652 numbering has permanent gaps (not every suffix was ever assigned), and for several manuals only the reference survives, not the physical document. Entries marked "n.d." or "?" have no located copy yet.
- **StuG classification criterion:** any suffix whose title reads *"Gepanzerte Selbstfahrlafette f. Sturmgeschütz"* or *"Sturmgeschütz 7,5 cm Kanone"* / *"StuG 40"* / *"StuH 42"*. Titles reading *"Panzerkampfwagen III"* or *"Panzerbefehlswagen"* belong to the tank / command vehicle.
- **Boundary manuals:** Section 2 (D 652/50a–c) needs the most care in translation, because a single document serves all three vehicles and mixes different Ausführung ranges per subsystem.
- **Maybach HL 120 TR/TRM engine:** had its own independent manuals (e.g. Heft 90 D type), transferred with the equipment; track these separately from the D 652 series.

## Sources & how to consult them

The D 652 series is fragmented across archives. No single catalogue is complete; cross-check.

### Alkmaar '40-'45 — primary source for this series

The most complete catalogue for D 652. It is a Google Sites site: navigation is by linked pages (no reliable internal search), organised first by manual family, then by numeric range.

- **Manuals home:** https://www.museumalkmaar40-45.nl/voorschriften-manuals
- **D 652 range (standard, unclassified):** https://www.museumalkmaar40-45.nl/voorschriften-manuals/d-d/d-652-655
- **D 652 classified versions (`+` / D+.):** https://www.museumalkmaar40-45.nl/voorschriften-manuals/d-d/d-500-652 — a separate page. A document may have both a standard and a classified entry; look for cross-notes like *"(Sehe auch D. 652/2+)"*.
- **"Newly added" page:** https://www.museumalkmaar40-45.nl/voorschriften-manuals/newly-added — the richest single page for D 652; lists /41, /41a, /43a, /44, /45, /46, /50a–c together with full titles. Useful when the range pages are slow to navigate.

**Entry format:** designation · vehicle · subsystem title · date · PDF availability with origin. PDF origins: **Bushmakov** (bushmakow.com), **NARA** (US National Archives microfilm), **BA** (Bundesarchiv), **BW** (Bundeswehr libraries). "PDF – Bushmakov" means a scan is available.

**Manual family key** (Alkmaar's own classification):
- **H.Dv.** — planned Army manuals; **H.Dv.g.** — classified planned
- **D.** — unplanned Army manuals *(our series)*; **D+.** — classified unplanned
- **L.Dv. / M.Dv.** — Air Force / Navy manuals

**Finding a specific number:** since there is no internal search, either go to the correct range page, or use Google directly, e.g. `site:museumalkmaar40-45.nl "D. 652/41a"`.

### superborg.com — secondary, weak for D 652

Official-style index by numeric range. Strong for other series (Panther D 655, Tiger D 656) but **incomplete for D 652**: lists only ~8 suffixes and jumps /41 → /45. Do not rely on it for the StuG chassis manuals — notably **D 652/41a is absent here**. Range page: https://www.superborg.com/d640.htm (D 640–659).

### Other archives

- **Bushmakow** (bushmakow.com) — restoration archive specialised in vehicle D-Vorschriften; source of several StuG scans.
- **Bundesarchiv / Archivportal-D** — original holdings under *BArch RHD 8, Waffentechnische D-Vorschriften, D 600–D 699 (Panzertruppe und Heeresmotorisierung)*.
- **WH-Dienstvorschriften.de** — large digital archive of Wehrmacht technical manuals.
