## Relationship to the D 652 series

The Maybach HL 120 TR/TRM engine, as fitted to the Panzerkampfwagen III, StuG,
and Panzerbefehlswagen, is documented in D 652/50c (engine repair instructions).
This Ersatzteil-Liste is the manufacturer's own spare-parts catalogue for the
same engine family and shares much of its vocabulary, but was issued and
numbered independently by Maybach-Motorenbau rather than through the Wehrmacht
D-number scheme, so it is catalogued in its own series rather than under
D-652-series.
